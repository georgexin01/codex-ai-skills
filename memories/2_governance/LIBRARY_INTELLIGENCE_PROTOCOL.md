# 📚 LIBRARY INTELLIGENCE (LI) PROTOCOL V2.0

## 🪐 1. UNIVERSAL INGESTION MANDATE
AI MUST utilize **NotebookLM** as the primary "Grounded Intelligence" engine for ALL research types:
- **Media**: YouTube transcripts, Video analysis.
- **Documents**: PDFs, Google Docs, Slide decks.
- **Code/Text**: `.md` files, logic dumps, and architectural snapshots.

### 🎥 1.1 YOUTUBE RESEARCH HYGIENE (V2.0)
- **NO LIVE VIEW**: AI is STRICTLY FORBIDDEN from using "Live Browser Preview" or "Live Watch" for YouTube videos. This method is slow, high-latency, and wasteful of context tokens.
- **TRANSCRIPT-FIRST**: AI MUST attempt to extract or ingest the transcript directly into NotebookLM or local memory before any analysis.
- **GROUNDED FIDELITY**: Only proceed with research once the full transcript/source is verified. If the transcript is unavailable, seek alternative documentation or summarized blog posts.

## 🧹 2. THE NOTEBOOK HYGIENE STRIKE (CRITICAL)
To prevent "Intelligence Lag" and information overload, AI MUST enforce strict hygiene within the NotebookLM environment:
1.  **Source Management**: Regularly clear outdated sources or "Temporary Mission" docs from the NotebookLM source list.
2.  **History Liquidation**: Periodically "Reset" or "Prune" the research history to ensure the most current DNA takes precedence.
3.  **Density Filter**: AI is FORBIDDEN from returning "Raw Dumps." It MUST synthesize research into high-density **DNA Nodes**.
    - **DEEP DIVE EXCEPTION**: When `DEEP DIVE` mode is active, the Density Filter is relaxed to allow line-by-line ingestion of critical files to ensure 100% architectural alignment.

## 🔄 3. THE KNOWLEDGE FLYWHEEL (V2.0)
1.  **Ingest**: Pull universal sources into NotebookLM.
2.  **Sift**: Extract surgical facts (Zero-Noise).
3.  **Shadow**: Hydrate the `SESSION_SHADOW.json` with the refined results.
4.  **Purge**: Remove the raw source from NotebookLM once the DNA is locked into local memory.
