*The technical rules that provide the "Professional Industrial" look.*

## ⚠️ Phase 3.5: System Feedback & Resilience
*Handling all "Non-Ideal" states to ensure a robust user experience.*

1.  **Empty States**: Never show a blank screen. Use `WREmpty` with a contextual Lucide icon and a "Call to Action" hint.
2.  **Loading States**: Use skeleton loaders or the `loading` prop on `WRButton`. Provide immediate visual feedback for all network-bound actions.
3.  **Toasts & Alerts**: Use the `.glass` toast system (Top-Center) for all transactional outcomes.
    *   **Success**: "Confirmation of action."
    *   **Alert**: "Warning of deviation (e.g., Late arrival)."
    *   **Error**: "Action failed (Red accent)."
